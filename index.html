A little bit about myself

I am an experienced and ambitious Front-end Developer with over 7 years commercial experience in a digital agency environment. I excell in both front-end and back-end development.

I develop with best practices in mind and adhere to W3C standards. Unfortunately not all browsers are created equal, so I utilise Progressive Enhancement where possible, to ensure that any website I work on displays perfectly in all of the evergreen browsers (Chrome, Firefox, and Safari).... and IE.

I believe that having a functional website is only part of the solution. Being found is just as important. Search Engine Optimisation is an integral part of my development process.
